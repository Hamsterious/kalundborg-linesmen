Our profile To top

Kalundborg Linesmen ApS, Established in 2001 has since then been serving most of the vessels calling at Kalundborg in connection with mooring and unmooring service.

Our team are available 24 hours around the clock within half an hour.

Besides mooring and unmooring, we are also serving the local shipping companies in connection with rigging of fenders used for STS (ship to ship transfer), positioning of barges in connection with loading of risers at the NKT Flexibels Terminal in Kalundborg. We are also supplying man power to Asnaesvaerket PowerStation in connection with handling of oil and discharging of Coal.

Kalundborg has in fact three different ports, each handling many different commodities, all sorts of bulk, and different sorts of liquid commodities:

1) Statoil Terminal belonging to the Statoil Refinery.

2) Statoil Refinery has a regular import of Crudeoil and Condensate from the Northsea, and export of various oil products to most of Norther Europe.

3) Asnaesvaerket PowerStation belonging to Dong Energy, besides import of coal, used for production of electricity, the powerstation are handling a large quantity of fuel oil.
